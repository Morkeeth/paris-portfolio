THE WAY OF PRODUCT
The Timeless Art of Building
Paris, Staff PM @ Ledger
1

The roadmap that can be followed is not the eternal roadmap.
The feature that can be named is not the limitless feature.

From nothing, the user's need arises.
From need, ten thousand solutions emerge.

Free from attachment to metrics,
you see the essence.
Caught in vanity metrics,
you see only the surface.

These two spring from the same source.
This mystery—the gateway to all understanding.

2

When we call a feature brilliant,
other features become dull.
When we praise speed,
the notion of deliberation is born.

Simplicity and complexity define each other.
User and builder create each other.
Problem and solution follow each other.

Therefore the product manager acts
without forcing,
teaches without preaching.
Features arise and she observes them.
Features fade and she lets them go.

She builds without claiming ownership.
Ships without seeking credit.
The work completes itself.

3

Do not glorify the rockstar engineer
and teams will not compete destructively.

Do not treasure viral growth
and users will not be exploited.

Do not display what others desire
and their hearts will not be disturbed.

The product manager leads by:
Emptying the backlog of noise.
Strengthening the team's foundation.
Releasing the need for certainty.
Embracing what emerges naturally.

7

Great products endure.
Eternal, they transcend trends and hype.
They last because
they do not serve the ego of their makers.

The product manager stays behind,
that is why she's ahead.
She is detached from the outcome,
thus at one with the user.

Through selfless action,
she is perfectly fulfilled.

11

Thirty features spoke together at the hub,
but it is the core use case
that makes the product useful.

We shape screens and flows,
but it is the white space between
that holds attention.

We work with code and pixels,
but the non-doing makes it work.

15

The ancient product masters were subtle,
mysterious, profound, responsive.

Because they are unfathomable,
we can only describe their manner:
Watchful, like crossing a winter stream.
Alert, like sensing danger.
Simple, like uncarved wood.
Open, like a valley.
Clear, like muddy water settling.

Who can wait quietly
while the mud settles?
Who can remain still
until the moment of action?

24

He who stands on tiptoe
doesn't stand firm.
He who ships hastily
doesn't ship far.

He who markets his brilliance
doesn't illuminate.
He who justifies his decisions
is never right.
He who boasts of success
achieves nothing lasting.

From the Tao's perspective,
these are like leftover food—
excess baggage.
They bring only disgust.

81

True words are not eloquent.
Eloquent words are not true.
Good products are not complex.
Complex products are not good.

The master knows without studying.
The master has without accumulating.

The more she does for users,
the more she has.
The more she gives to the team,
the greater her abundance.

The way of product is to build without harming.
The way of the product manager is to act
without contending.

Paris, France • 2025

Staff Product Manager @ Ledger
