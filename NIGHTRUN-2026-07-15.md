**Bug 2 — hydration mismatch on /fable, root-caused.** The Equalizer's `--ad` differed
between server and client at the 10th decimal (`-0.7752093506969686s` vs
`-0.7752093507078825s`) while `--d`, `--h` and `--bar-c` matched exactly. Cause:
`rnd()` (`fable:48`) is built on `Math.sin`, which the ECMAScript spec permits to
differ in its last bits between engines — Node and Chrome disagree. Fixed at the
source by rounding `rnd`'s output: everything downstream is `+ - * /` only, which is
IEEE-exact and identical on every engine. One line, kills the whole class.
